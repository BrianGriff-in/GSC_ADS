  • • Manual Paper Gate Checks: High risk of falsification, error, or slow archival processing.

03. High-Priority Safety Overlay System

Critical operations inside a busy dormitory dashboard need foolproof controls. We designed a layered pop-up structure to prevent accidental triggers.

Confirmation Z-Index Prioritization

We re-layered visual hierarchies to push modal overlays dynamically into the absolute front of standard administrative modal wrappers.

Confirmation Modals: z-index: 1500 (Priority)
